About Me

Greetings! My name is Jonathan Gomez and although my background is not entirely in computers, I love coding. Originally, I went to school for opticianry. I got my degree and passed the Florida state board becoming a licensed optician. I did that for about 8 years until I decided I was ready for a new challenge. I resigned from my job and joined the UNC Charlotte full-stack coding bootcamp full time. The program was intensive but I enjoyed every minute of it. I genuinely love learning new things and putting my problem solving skills to the test. That's why programming is such a perfect match for me.

I look forward to sharpening my abilities by continuing to self-teach with online courses and hopefully use those abilites in the real world once I land a job. I know my lack of programming experience is a sore spot for many employers but there are reasons it can actually work to their benefit. For one, I have worked 8 years in customer service which has taught me patience, teamwork, and excellent communication skills. Secondly, I know that every company likes things done differently, since I'm something of a blank slate, I won't bring in any old habits that your company would disapprove of. In conclusion, I'm really excited to begin my career as a developer and would love a chance to further discuss our compatibility at an interview.
